Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

           
       
         

Course Overview

         

Learn the process of **optimizing website content and technical structure** to achieve top rankings on search engines like Google and Bing. This course covers keyword research, link building, technical SEO audits, and content strategy to drive organic (free) traffic.

         

This is a foundational skill for digital marketers, website owners, and content writers looking to maximize online visibility.
