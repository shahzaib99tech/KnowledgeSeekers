IELTS Preparation and English Language

           
       
         

Course Overview

         

This comprehensive course is designed to develop all four core English skills—**Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking**—with a specific focus on achieving target scores in the IELTS exam (Academic and General Training). We emphasize grammar, vocabulary building, and effective test-taking strategies.

         

Ideal for students planning to study abroad, immigration purposes, or simply enhancing professional communication skills.

       
               
         
           

Key Details

           
                 
  • Duration: Minimum 3 Months
    •              
  • Classes: 3 Days per Week (Physical & Online)
    •              
  • Focus: IELTS Academic/General & Fluency
