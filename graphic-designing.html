Professional Graphic Designing

           
       
         

Course Overview

         

Master the tools and principles required to create visually stunning and effective designs. This course focuses on practical applications using **Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign** for branding, marketing materials, and digital art.

         

Develop a strong understanding of color theory, typography, and composition to launch a career as a freelance designer or agency professional.

       
               
         
           

Key Details

           
                 
  • Duration: Minimum 3 Months
    •              
  • Classes: 3 Days per Week (Physical & Online)
    •              
  • Focus: Branding, Print & Digital Design
    •              
  • Software: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator

Detailed Curriculum

           
               
         

Module 1: Design Fundamentals & Photoshop

         
               
  • Principles of design, color theory, and visual hierarchy.

  • Mastering photo retouching, manipulation, and digital painting.

  • Creating high-quality social media graphics and banners.

Module 2: Vector Graphics & Branding (Illustrator)

         
               
  • Creating custom logos, icons, and vector illustrations.
    •            
  • Developing complete brand identity guidelines.
    •            
  • Designing business cards, brochures, and print layouts.
