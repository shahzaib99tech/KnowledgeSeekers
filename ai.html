Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI)

           
       
         

Course Overview

         

This introductory course provides a practical understanding of **Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning (ML), and large language models (LLMs)**. Learn how to effectively use tools like ChatGPT, Midjourney, and other AI platforms to automate tasks, generate content, and solve complex problems in business and programming.

         

This is essential training for modern professionals seeking a competitive edge through emerging technology.

       
               
         
           

Key Details

           
                 
  • Duration: Minimum 3 Months
    •              
  • Classes: 3 Days per Week (Physical & Online)
    •              
  • Focus: Prompt Engineering, ML Concepts, Automation
    •              
  • Tools: ChatGPT, Bard, Midjourney/DALL-E
