WordPress and CMS Development

           
       
         

Course Overview

         

Learn to build dynamic, professional websites and blogs using the world's most popular Content Management System, **WordPress**. This course covers installation, theme customization, plugin integration, security, and performance optimization without writing complex code.

         

Gain practical skills to create e-commerce sites, portfolios, and business websites quickly for yourself or for clients.

       
               
         
           

Key Details

           
                 
  • Duration: Minimum 3 Months

  • Classes: 3 Days per Week (Physical & Online)
    •              
  • Focus: WordPress, Hosting, Elementor/Builders
    •
